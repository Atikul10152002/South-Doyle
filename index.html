We are Team Soknorobo

We are South Doyle's Team Soknorobo, Winner of the Battle for the Bluegrass 2017 Robotics Competition at Lynn Camp High School in Corbin, Kentucky. Winners of The "Highest Rookie Seed" award and "Rookie All Star Award" at the 2017 Smokey Mountain Regional Tournament.

South-Doyle High School

South-Doyle High School is a Southern Association of Colleges and Schools' accredited high school in Knoxville, Tennessee, operated by the Knox County Schools school district
